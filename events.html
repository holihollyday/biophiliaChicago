Upcoming Events

As part of this project’s ongoing effort to re-establish a connection between humans and nature in the 21st Century, we hold events and workshops that focus on nature walks, artmaking, and birdwatching by asking participants to shift their mindset in order to be more receptive to sensory stimuli from nature. This creates an experiential and oftentimes intimate knowledge of your immediate and natural surroundings, if that is a small park, a tree-lined boulevard, or the waterfront.

These events are open to any and all interested parties - we firmly believe in creating inclusive spaces to share our thoughts, ideas, and artwork. All artistic mediums and modes of expression are welcome: drawing, painting, photography, writing, poetry, performance, sculpture, installation art, land art, and digital art, just to name a few!
